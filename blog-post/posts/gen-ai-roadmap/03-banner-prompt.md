## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** A single, small, clearly-lit path glowing among many larger, dimmer, overgrown branching paths — the one deliberately chosen route standing out against the much bigger cluttered map it was cut down from.

**Prompt (universal base):**
A minimal editorial illustration of one short, brightly lit path cutting straight through a dense, dim network of overgrown branching paths and nodes. The dense network fades into shadow at the edges; the single clear path is the only sharp, illuminated line in the frame. Dark, moody, atmospheric background. Clean geometric line work. Wide 16:9 composition with negative space around the glowing path.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
