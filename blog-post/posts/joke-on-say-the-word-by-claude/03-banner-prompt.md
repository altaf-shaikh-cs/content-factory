## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** A single spoken word crossing an empty distance and completing an unfinished shape on the other side, like a circuit closing the instant it's said.

**Prompt (universal base):**
Dark editorial illustration, wide 16:9 format. On the left, a small glowing speech mark or single dot of light, minimal and quiet, floating against a deep charcoal background. On the right, a geometric form that is almost complete but missing one final segment, like an open circuit or an unfinished loop, waiting. A thin, faint line of light travels from the speech mark toward the incomplete shape, about to close the gap. Enormous negative space between the two elements emphasizes the distance a single word is about to cross. Clean, minimal, atmospheric, editorial. No text, no code, no people, no UI elements.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
